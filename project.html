Project Overview

For RESECON 490AI, I completed an industry research project that uses data and recent reports to analyze how artificial intelligence and digital tools are transforming a specific industry. The report examines current conditions, employment patterns, and how AI may change the demand for different kinds of work.

The goal of the project is to practice reading evidence from multiple sources, interpret simple statistics, and connect them to concepts from AI in Economics: technological change, labor demand and supply, and skill requirements. The written report is the primary deliverable; later in the semester, I may add a short slide deck that summarizes the key points in a more visual format.

In class, this project also serves as a way to experiment with AI tools:

  • Drafting and revising text with AI assistance while keeping final judgment my own.
  • Using AI to help polish the structure and style of the website you are viewing now.
  • Reflecting on where AI is helpful, and where careful human interpretation is still essential.
